Egypt Packages

Egypt Packages is Hurghada-based travel agency and has been creating amazing Egyptian travel experiences for customers since 2005. Since then, we have grown across the country by maintaining our excellent customer service and belief that your trip should be memorable for all the right reasons. Reasons to choose us include:

Quality checked accommodation: to ensure you have a good night's sleep wherever you stay, every accommodation is tested by our travel consultants. Travel guide: our team's insider travel tips will make your visit special.


Tailor-made itinerary: we personalise the itinerary with your needs in mind, so that you have the vacation of a lifetime. 24/7 helpline: for your peace of mind, there's always a Egypt-based travel consultant on the other end of our 24/7 helpline.


We aim to have a positive impact on our destinations: Egypt Packages' social responsibility is to support and strengthen tourism in all the destinations we promote and to which we sell tours. This way we play an active part in promoting cultural diversity and in strengthening the local economies.


We respect our natural environment: Tourism plays a fundamental role in many of our destinations' economies and can impact their natural environments. That is the very reason why we encourage employees to use environmentally friendly, cost-efficient and healthy means of transportation
